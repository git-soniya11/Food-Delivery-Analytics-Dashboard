🍔 Food Delivery Analytics Dashboard

> An interactive **Power BI Business Intelligence Dashboard** designed to analyze food delivery operations, customer behavior, restaurant performance, and key business metrics through dynamic visualizations and actionable insights.

---

📖 Project Overview

The **Food Delivery Analytics Dashboard** is a Power BI project that transforms raw food delivery data into meaningful business insights. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor performance, identify trends, evaluate customer retention, and make data-driven decisions using interactive reports and KPI visualizations.

This project demonstrates practical skills in **data cleaning, data modeling, DAX, Power Query, and dashboard design**, making it suitable as a portfolio project for Data Analyst roles.

---

🎯 Business Objectives

The dashboard was developed to answer key business questions, including:

* How is the business performing over time?
* Which restaurants generate the highest revenue?
* What are the monthly order and revenue trends?
* How many customers are being retained versus lost?
* Which payment methods are most frequently used?
* How efficient are delivery operations?
* Which customer segments contribute the most revenue?
